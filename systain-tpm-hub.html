SYSTAIN® Third Party Maintenance FAQs

What is Systain® Third-Party Maintenance (TPM)?

Systain® is OSI Global's third-party maintenance service that provides expert support for networking, server, and storage infrastructure — helping organizations extend hardware life and reduce reliance on OEM contracts.

Can OSI Global Systain® replace OEM support for Cisco, Dell, Supermicro, Juniper, and other major vendors?

Yes. OSI Global Systain® is designed to support enterprise hardware from major vendors, including environments where OEM support is no longer cost-effective or has reached end of life (EOL).

Does OSI Global Systain® support End-of-Life (EOL) and End-of-Support (EOS) equipment?

Yes. OSI Global Systain includes EOL and EOS hardware support, allowing organizations to keep existing infrastructure running reliably without forced refresh cycles.

What SLA options are available with OSI Global Systain®?

OSI Global Systain® offers flexible SLA options, including Next Business Day (NBD), 4-hour response, and 4-hour response with on-site technician services — depending on coverage needs and location.

Is 24/7 support included with OSI Global Systain®?

Yes. OSI Global Systain® provides 24x7x365 TAC coverage to support mission-critical environments at any time.

Who provides technical support for OSI Global Systain®?

Support requests are handled by experienced Level 2 and Level 3 engineers, backed by an accelerated escalation process. At OSI Global, you get real humans to talk to — no wasted time with L1 scripted support.

Does OSI Global Systain® include hardware replacement services?

Yes. Hardware replacement is included to help minimize downtime when component failures occur, backed by our industry-leading SLAs.

How does OSI Global Systain® help organizations reduce infrastructure costs?

By extending hardware lifespan, supporting EOL equipment, and offering flexible SLAs, OSI Global Systain® helps organizations reduce maintenance spend and avoid unnecessary capital refreshes.
