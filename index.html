OSI Global FAQs

Who is OSI Global?+
OSI Global is a privately owned IT infrastructure provider that helps enterprises manage, extend the life of, and retire their hardware through third-party maintenance (TPM), optical solutions, professional data center services, and IT asset disposition (ITAD).
What types of IT services does OSI Global provide?+
OSI Global supports the full infrastructure lifecycle, including third-party maintenance (TPM), enterprise hardware sourcing, optical transceivers, and professional data center services such as relocation, smart hands, IT deployment, infrastructure audits, ITAD, and certified data destruction.
What is third-party maintenance (TPM), and why do organizations use it?+
Third-party maintenance is an alternative to OEM support that helps organizations maintain and extend the life of networking, server, and storage equipment. Many teams use TPM to gain flexibility, control support costs, and reduce total cost of ownership.
Does OSI Global support enterprise networking and optical infrastructure?+
Yes. OSI Global supports enterprise networking -- switches, routers, and access points -- and optical infrastructure with fully compatible optical transceivers, cables, and connectivity solutions designed to work across major OEM platforms.
What service coverage and SLAs does OSI Global offer?+
OSI Global offers flexible coverage options, including 24/7/365 support and SLA choices designed for business-critical environments -- such as next business day replacement and rapid response services.
Does OSI Global offer data center and IT professional services?+
Yes. OSI Global provides IT professional services to support infrastructure planning, hardware deployment, and data center initiatives -- working alongside internal teams and existing vendors. Services include data center relocation, smart hands, IT deployment, wireless solutions, infrastructure audits, asset recovery, data destruction, and staff augmentation.
What is IT asset disposition (ITAD), and does OSI Global provide it?+
IT asset disposition (ITAD) is the secure and compliant retirement of IT equipment, including data security and responsible handling of decommissioned assets. OSI Global is R2 recycling certified and offers ITAD services to help organizations reduce risk and recover value.
Who does OSI Global typically work with?+
OSI Global works with enterprise organizations across industries that rely on complex, mission-critical IT infrastructure and need reliable, cost-effective lifecycle support.
